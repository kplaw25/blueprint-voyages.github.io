About Me

Hi, I’m the planner behind Blueprint Voyages! I started this business out of a passion for travel and a knack for organizing complex, unforgettable trips. After planning my own wedding and a month-long trip across four European countries with 28 people, I realized how many people dream of travel but feel overwhelmed by logistics. I’m here to turn your ideas into a crystal-clear travel blueprint — so you can focus on making memories.

What Makes Me Different

  • Analytical & Organized: I combine project management skills with a love for detail to create practical, streamlined plans.
  • Travel Passion: I genuinely love helping others experience the joy of travel and life-changing adventures.
  • Real Experience: From personal multi-country travel to group coordination, I’ve done this firsthand.
  • Non-Agent Objectivity: I don’t earn commission – my only goal is building the best plan for YOU.
  • Beautiful Documentation: You’ll receive visually intuitive PDFs and mobile-friendly versions for ease on the go.
  • Flexible Itineraries: I believe a good plan includes space to breathe and enjoy spontaneous moments.

What I Offer

  • Custom day-by-day itineraries (PDF + mobile-friendly)
  • Travel booking checklist with helpful links
  • Lodging, dining, activities, and transportation recs
  • Budget and group coordination spreadsheets
  • Custom packing lists & destination-specific tips
  • Event planning for birthdays, bachelorettes, and more
  • Google Maps for visual planning
