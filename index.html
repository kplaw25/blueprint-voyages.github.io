Dream Trips Are Amazing — Planning Them Should Be Too

Big trips, especially overseas, can be overwhelming. You want to savor every moment, but the planning is full of tough questions:

  • Where’s the best area to stay so you’re close to what you love?
  • What’s the smartest way to get from place to place?
  • How do you find authentic experiences and avoid tourist traps?
  • Which passes should I buy for my travel needs?
  • How many days should I stay at each location?

I’ve been there — my first big trip had me juggling all these questions and more. What I found is that I love the research, organizing every detail, and making sure everyone has exactly what they need in one clear, stress-free plan.

How I Help You Travel Better

Whether you’re planning a weekend escape or a multi-week adventure, I’ll work with you to:

  • Research the best neighborhoods, transportation options, and activities tailored to your interests
  • Keep all your confirmations, addresses, and schedules organized in one easy-to-use format
  • Share tips from my own European travels — like the best apps for trains and public transit
  • Walk you through your trip plan step-by-step, so you’re confident and excited

You book everything yourself, but I stay in the loop to make sure your plan stays on track and stress-free.
