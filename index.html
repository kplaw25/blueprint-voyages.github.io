Dream Trips Are Fantastic — But Planning Them Isn’t

We all love to imagine the perfect getaway. But turning that dream into reality takes endless time and research — time most of us don’t have. That’s where I come in.

  • Which flights or trains actually make sense for my route?
  • Should I stay in a hotel or Airbnb — and in which neighborhood?
  • How do I choose between the 50 identical-looking tours?
  • Which restaurants are worth my time and money?
  • Do I need a Swiss Travel Pass, or just a Swiss Day Pass?
  • Which mountain hike is best for moderate effort and stunning views?
  • What’s the cheapest way to get phone service abroad?
  • Where is that lost confirmation email for my flight, hotel, or excursion?

Instead of drowning in the details, you get to dream and decide what excites you most. I’ll handle the time-consuming research, organization, and coordination — so all you have to do is book the final activities and enjoy the trip of a lifetime with everything at your fingertips.

How I Help You Travel Better

I make sure you spend less time stressing and more time experiencing. Here’s how:

  • Curate the best transport routes, accommodations, and activities to match your travel style
  • Organize all confirmations, schedules, and must-know details into simple, ready-to-use documents
  • Provide clear comparisons (so you don’t waste hours choosing between 20 similar tours)
  • Give you insider tips from real travels — like how to skip lines, which apps to use, and how to save money

You make the final bookings. I make the process easy, exciting, and completely stress-free.
