Tailored Travel Planning Services

Every trip is different. Whether you're starting with just an idea or already have pieces in place, my planning services are flexible and personalized to fit your needs. From small getaways to large group adventures, I take the time to understand your vision and make it a reality.

Every client begins with a free consultation call where you can share your goals, timeline, and preferences. After the call, I’ll provide a custom quote based on the complexity, length, and scope of your trip.

Fill out the interest form on the contact page to get started!

What I Offer

  • Trip Conceptualization: Help you choose where to go, based on time, season, and travel style.
  • Destination & Lodging Guidance: Recommend ideal cities, neighborhoods, and specific hotels or Airbnbs based on your trip goals.
  • Flight Assistance: Research and guide you to best flight options, including alerts on pricing trends.
  • Activity & Experience Planning: Curate activities based on your preferences, and contact providers to check availability so you can book confidently.
  • Itinerary Documentation: Provide you with a clean, organized travel document containing addresses, directions, booking details, and daily plans.
  • Meal & Event Planning: Offer dining recommendations and help plan special occasions or group meals.
  • Destination Research: Provide tips, cultural insights, and must-know travel information for each location.
  • Packing & Travel Tips: Customized packing lists and travel prep guides tailored to your destinations and activities.
  • Personalized Meetings: Meet virtually or in-person (locally) as needed throughout the planning process.

Note: I am not a travel agent and do not make bookings. I equip you with everything needed so you can stay in control and book confidently.

Pricing

Because no two trips are the same, pricing is custom based on the amount of time and support needed. After our free consult call, you'll receive a quote outlining a flat planning fee.

Whether you're planning a romantic getaway, a month-long European adventure, or a family trip with multiple stops — I’m here to help every step of the way.
