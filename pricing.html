Start with a Free Consultation Call

Everyone starts with a free, no-pressure consultation call. It’s a chance for us to connect, talk through your trip, and see if we’re a good fit. There’s no obligation to move forward, and nothing is locked in from that chat.

Interested in working together? Reach out through the contact page and we’ll set up a time to talk and create a personalized plan and pricing structure that works for your unique trip.

Flexible Pricing

The prices below serve as a general guide. Final pricing may be higher or lower depending on the level of customization, trip length, and complexity. Once we talk through your trip we can decide a price that makes sense.

Pricing Base Framework

Trip Length 1–4 People 5–10 People 10+ People
Weekend (1–3 days) $250 $325 $400
Short Trip (4–7 days) $375 $475 $575
Standard Trip (8–14 days) $525 $650 $775
Extended Trip (15+ days) $800 $950 $1,100

Add-On Options

  • Special Occasion Planning (honeymoon, anniversary, proposal, birthday bash, etc.): +$150–$500
  • Last-Minute Planning (less than 2 weeks' notice): +$100 flat
  • Other Custom Requests (extra research, event planning, budget help, or anything else you need): Just ask! We’ll discuss if additional costs apply based on your unique request.

Notes & Flexibility

  • All packages assume full-service planning support unless otherwise discussed.
  • If you've already made progress on your itinerary, we can reduce the cost—just ask!
  • Pricing is for planning only. You'll book your own travel and accommodations.
  • There are no hidden fees and pricing is confirmed before planning begins.
